GLOSSARY

Glossary

Brown shirts

The name given to the members of the German National Socialist Party, because of the colour of the uniform adopted by the party in 1925, has often been limited to the SA (paramilitary formations of the Nazi party).

Generation Z

The term designates the generation of people born between 1997 and 2010. It succeeds the Y generation and precedes the Alpha generation. It is defined as a generation born when digital communications were already well established in society.

Arcade Games

Paid game played on public machines; especially action video game.

Music noise

English noise music brings together various musical genres and is characterized by the assembly of sounds commonly perceived as non musical (noises, non-harmonious sounds, etc.)

Post-apocalyptic

Which, in the anticipation stories, is located after a disaster that devastated the Earth.

Totalitarianism

Political system in which the state, in the name of an ideology, exerts a control over the totality of individual activities.
