About

To start this first year MMI, we have carried out a personal project on the theme «notes for later», inspired by the famous song by Orelsan. It is precisely on the theme of time that I have focused my realization: the links between present, past and future. These relationships are complex, sometimes positive but often disturbing. It is a way of translating the oppressive aspect of time and fear of the future. To illustrate this theme, I chose references from the artistic world: music, films, sculptures, drawings… This is an area that particularly touches me, especially in its mysterious dimension.
